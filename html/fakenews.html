Debunking: Fake News

Fact vs. Opinion

Believe it or not: not everything on the Internet is true! There are a plethora of "news" sites that may seem trustworthy and reliable, but actually spread misinformation to the public. A lot of the information that's readily available to view online can be biased or misleading. To catch these sites, you must look at how exactly they present their information. Do they use loaded terms and make generalizations or do they provide you with accurate facts and statistics? Languages cues are imperative in order to detect the real from the fake!
